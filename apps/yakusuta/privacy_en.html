Privacy Policy for Yakusuta

This Privacy Policy defines the handling of user information, including personal information, in Yakusuta (hereinafter referred to as "this app").

1. Information Collected and Purpose of Use

This app may collect and use the following information to provide better services.

1-1. Use of Camera

This app uses the device's camera for text reading (OCR) and object detection.

  • Image data captured/obtained by the camera is processed only on the device and used for translation and text generation.
  • No images are sent to external servers.

1-2. Advertising

This app uses third-party advertising providers (Google AdMob) to deliver advertisements.
These providers may use information about your visits to this app and other websites (such as cookies, advertising IDs, device IDs, and location info) to display relevant advertisements.

For more details on how Google AdMob handles your information and how to opt-out, please refer to the following links:

  • Google Policies and Terms: Technologies
  • Google Privacy Policy

1-3. Usage Analytics

To improve quality, we may collect non-personally identifiable usage data (crash reports, frequency of use, etc.) using analytics tools provided by Google (e.g., Firebase Analytics).

1-4. Translation Models

Translation models are downloaded upon first use.
By downloading these models, the offline translation feature becomes available.

2. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time in response to changes in laws and regulations.
